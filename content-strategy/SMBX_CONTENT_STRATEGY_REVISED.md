# THE 4 PROBLEMS WE SOLVE (For M&A Professionals)

## 1. "I spend 40 hours building a CIM. Yulia does it in 30 minutes."
The time problem. CIMs, valuations, financial models, buyer lists, teasers — all the deliverables that eat analyst hours. Yulia generates them from a conversation.

## 2. "I screen 3,000 deals to close 1. Most should have been dead in 90 seconds."
The screening problem. Deal flow is infinite. Time is not. The Rundown scores any deal on 7 dimensions in 60 seconds. Kill the 85% that will never close. Spend your time on the 15% that might.

## 3. "I manage 15 active deals and nothing is organized."
The pipeline problem. DD checklists across 8 deals. LOIs in various stages. Buyer communications for 12 mandates. Deal rooms for every client. One platform instead of 40 spreadsheets.

## 4. "My clients can't afford me. So I turn away 60% of deal flow."
The economics problem. A $2M deal doesn't justify a $50K engagement. But with Yulia generating the CIM, valuation, and buyer list, that $2M deal becomes profitable at $149/month. Zero deals turned away.
