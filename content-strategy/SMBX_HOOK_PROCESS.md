# THE 3-STEP PROCESS

## Step 1: START WITH WHAT PRACTITIONERS NEED

Not what business owners wonder about. What M&A pros need to do their job faster.

Ask: "What takes this person 40 hours that should take 40 minutes? What costs them $50K that should cost $149? What do they do manually that could be automated?"

**Sources:**
- SearchFunder threads: what searchers complain about
- Broker forums: what eats their time
- PE associate conversations: what's painful about deal production
- Your own experience running deals

**The output is a PAIN POINT, not a topic.**

Examples:
- Brokers spend 40 hours on a CIM
- Searchers burn $85K on busted diligence
- PE associates spend 2 days on an IC memo
- Solo brokers turn away $5M EBITDA deals

## Step 2: FRAME IT AS PRODUCT

Take the pain point and show what Yulia does about it.

**Formula: [Pain in specific hours/dollars] → [Yulia result in specific hours/dollars]**

- "CIM takes 40 hours → Yulia: 30 minutes. Same 30 pages."
- "$180K busted costs → The Rundown catches every one in 90 seconds."
- "IC memo: 2 days → Yulia: 15 minutes. Same output."
- "$5M deal unprofitable → Profitable at $149/month."

**The test:** Does the hook contain a BEFORE number and an AFTER number? If not, add them.

## Step 3: BACK IT UP WITH THE OUTPUT

Show the deliverable. Not explain the concept.

**THE WALKTHROUGH** — slide by slide through a CIM, valuation, or deal score
→ Best for: Time hooks (T1-T10). Carousel format.

**THE COMPARISON** — before vs after, old way vs new way, cost vs cost
→ Best for: Economics hooks (E1-E7). Carousel or single image.

**THE PRACTITIONER STORY** — "A broker used Yulia to..." with specific metrics
→ Best for: Adoption phase (Weeks 5+). Carousel.

**THE DATA** — aggregate platform stats, founding member metrics
→ Best for: Flywheel phase (Weeks 11+). Single image or carousel.

# QUALITY CHECK — 4 QUESTIONS

1. **Does it show what Yulia DOES?** If it teaches an M&A concept instead, rewrite.
2. **Is there a time or cost comparison?** "40 hours → 30 minutes" or "$150K → $149/month." If not, add one.
3. **Would a broker/PE associate stop scrolling?** If it sounds like content for a business owner, it's wrong audience.
4. **Does it end with smbx.ai?** Every post. Every time. No exceptions.
