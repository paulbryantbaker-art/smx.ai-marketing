# RULES

1. **Never post the same content to the same group twice in 4 weeks.** Hooks recycle, copy doesn't.
2. **Tier 1 groups get the strongest hooks.** The CIM speed, the IB comparison, the Rundown demos.
3. **Tier 3-4 groups get the value/outcome hooks.** $2.6M hidden, $52M vs $32M, exit paths.
4. **Adapt the copy slightly per group.** The post to Investment Bankers Group uses IB language. The post to Business Owners uses outcome language. Same core message, different frame.
5. **Monday Slot 1 and Slot 2 are ALWAYS Tier 1 groups.** Our buyers live there.
6. **Personal feed gets EVERY post.** Groups get the adaptation. Personal feed gets the full version.
