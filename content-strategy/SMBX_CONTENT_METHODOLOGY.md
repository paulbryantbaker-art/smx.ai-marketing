# smbx.ai — CONTENT CREATION METHODOLOGY
## How to write any post for M&A professionals
## Updated: April 2026

---

# THE FORMULA

Every post follows ONE structure:

**[What takes too long / costs too much] → [What Yulia does instead] → [Specific time/cost result] → [CTA]**

---

# WRITING A POST IN 10 MINUTES

1. **Pick the hook from the bank** — the pain point + the Yulia result (2 min)
2. **Write the body** — show the output, the comparison, or the practitioner story (5 min)
3. **Add the CTA** — phase-appropriate call to action (1 min)
4. **Write the slides** — Slide 1: hook. Slides 2-4: proof. Slide 5: CTA + smbx.ai (2 min)

---

# POST BODY FORMATS

## THE OUTPUT SHOWCASE (Weeks 1-4)
Show what Yulia produces. List the deliverable contents. State the time.

Example structure:
- "CIM: 32 pages. Here's what's in it: [list]. Generated in 30 minutes from a 20-minute conversation."
- "Financial model: 5-year DCF, LBO returns, sensitivities. 4 minutes. Here's the output."

## THE PRACTITIONER STORY (Weeks 5-8)
A real (or composite) M&A professional using Yulia. Specific metrics.

Example structure:
- "A broker ran 4 CIMs last week. 3 hours total. Client's attorney asked who prepared it."
- "A PE associate built 3 IC memos in 45 minutes. Committee couldn't tell the difference."

## THE DATA PROOF (Weeks 9-13)
Aggregate founding member metrics. Platform stats.

Example structure:
- "482 CIMs generated. Average: 34 minutes. 1,847 deals scored. Average: 4.1 minutes."
- "Broker capacity: 10 mandates → 18. Revenue per broker: +85%."

---

# VOICE

Sounds like: a deal professional who's used every tool and found one that actually works. Direct. Specific. Numbers, not adjectives.

Does NOT sound like:
- ❌ A marketing team ("We're excited to announce...")
- ❌ An AI company ("Our proprietary AI engine leverages...")
- ❌ A business coach ("5 tips for better M&A outcomes...")
- ❌ A consultant ("In today's dynamic M&A landscape...")

---

# SLIDE STRUCTURE (For carousels)

| Slide | Purpose | Design |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 1: Hook/Cover | The headline number. Stop the scroll. | Dark bg. Large typography. One stat. |
| 2: Content | What's in the deliverable / the before picture | Light bg. List or breakdown. |
| 3: Content | The proof / the comparison / the deeper analysis | Dark bg. Side-by-side or data. |
| 4: Content | The outcome / the practitioner result / the math | Light bg. Specific numbers. |
| 5: CTA | Price + action + smbx.ai | Dark bg. Clean. Bold price. URL. |

For single images: One card with the headline stat, one supporting line, smbx.ai.

---

# $5M EBITDA FLOOR

Every deal example: $5M EBITDA minimum. Same industries (HVAC, plumbing, dental, IT, landscaping, manufacturing, services) but at scale. A PE associate managing a billion-dollar fund should never dismiss the content because of deal size.
